Iterative Design

Creating an app design for a startup – Statiq

View Full Project

Problem Statement

Given only the concept/business idea of the startup, without looking at their design/app, how can we design the most usable interface for it from scratch?

Overview

In this project, I explored how users interact with an interface that is accessible to the public, and create personas & storyboards based on my observation and interviews with users.

Specifically, my target interface is a ticketing kiosk located near the gates of every metro station in Taipei, Taiwan.

Key Conclusions

  • Interview questions are a key to getting insight on user motives, goals, and challenges

  • In order to improve an interface, we must understand what it's currently lacking, i.e. its key challenges

  • Since emotions, characteristics & goals play a huge role into how users use & understand interfaces, storyboard allows us to document a generalized user journey for a group of users (or for a persona), and makes it easier to see what challenges these user have in common.
